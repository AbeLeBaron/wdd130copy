Attractions

Outside

Gamble house offers many attractions that are sure to keep everyone occupied. This house is on a dead end street and it is sooo quiet and so dark out at night for star gazing on the balcony. The yard is so big, you'll never finish exploring all her secrets. We love grabbing the binoculars and catching the daily action on the bay. I just love the soft blanket of fog that hangs on the trees across the bay every morning. The pool is heated!!!!

Inside

The living room is set up for family movie night with a sectional and TV. There is a kitchen with a breakfast nook and a separate dining room with a big table. And don't forget the game room to keep the teenagers happy, and a decent toy closet for children. Impress your kids with your old school pac-man skills! Or give them (and you) and break and let them play the Wii-U. Upstairs there are three bedrooms with beds for 8.
