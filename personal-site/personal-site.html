About Us

We are local owners who are invested in this community. Our goal is to make out customers also fall in love with this area just as we have and give them a break with people you love! Enjoy the wrap around porch and two balconies over looking Port Gamble Bay. In the evenings enjoy beautiful sunsets over the forest on the other side of the bay and in the morning fall in love with the fog that clings to the trees across the water. Explore the shore down the steps and harvest some oysters for dinner!
